Using the TI‑83

Overview

Using the TI‑83 for Algebra II is a step‑by‑step manual designed to help students master the essential functions of the TI‑83 and TI‑84 graphing calculators. The guide focuses on the calculator skills most frequently used in Algebra II and precalculus courses, emphasizing clarity, reproducibility, and confidence‑building through structured procedures.

Topics include evaluating expressions, graphing and analyzing functions, solving equations and systems, exploring tables, and working with statistical lists. Each procedure is broken into small, mechanical steps so learners can follow along easily, whether they are using the calculator in class or as a reference while working independently.
