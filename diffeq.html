Differential Equations

Overview

An Introduction to Differential Equations is written for engineering students taking their first course in differential equations. The text blends classical analytical methods with modern computational techniques, giving students both the theoretical foundation and the practical tools needed for real‑world engineering problems.

The book covers core topics in ordinary differential equations, introduces essential ideas from partial differential equations, and presents numerical methods for approximating solutions to both single equations and systems. Emphasis is placed on intuition, modeling, and applications that reflect how engineers actually use differential equations in practice.
