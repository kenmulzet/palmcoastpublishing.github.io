Precalculus

Overview

A Student’s Approach to Precalculus is designed for high school and college learners preparing for or currently enrolled in a precalculus course. The text develops the foundational ideas that support later study in calculus, engineering, and the sciences, with an emphasis on conceptual understanding and problem‑solving fluency.

The book covers essential topics including functions and their transformations, trigonometry, conic sections, and sequences and series. Each chapter moves from guided examples to independent reasoning, helping students build confidence and mathematical maturity.
